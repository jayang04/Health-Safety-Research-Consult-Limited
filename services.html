Our Services

We conduct safety climate, well-being, and psychosocial risks research at your workplace. Our comprehensive services provide evidence-based solutions to improve worker health, well-being, and productivity.

Safety Climate Research

Our Safety Climate tool serves as a key indicator of safety outcomes, highlighting the relationship between safety decisions and production objectives. Safety climate reflects the prevailing safety conditions at a specific moment in time.

People at Work Assessment

The People at Work tool provides employers with a 5-step process to help leaders identify, assess, and manage risks to psychological health in the workplace, including national benchmarks and practical guidance.

Psychosocial Risk Assessment

Detailed assessment tools that examine workplace psychosocial hazards such as bullying, harassment, or workload stressors, measuring health and business productivity outcomes.

OHS System Evaluation

We evaluate your current health & safety policies, practices and procedures, and update your Occupational Health & Safety Management System under current legislation and policy.

Research & Consultancy

Experienced staff carry out research, consultancy and development projects as commissioned, with commercial levels of confidentiality assured and negotiable scope-based pricing.

Prevention Programs

We target areas where there is evidence of disproportionate number of injuries or fatalities (such as musculoskeletal injuries) and develop people-centered interventions.

Our Approach

1. Discovery

We start by understanding your unique challenges and goals.

2. Analysis

Our team conducts in-depth research and analysis.

3. Implementation

We help you implement tailored solutions and strategies.

4. Evaluation

We assess the impact of our interventions to ensure success.
