About

Jay ZX is the Web Developer at HEALTH&SAFETY RESEARCH CONSULT LIMITED, responsible for creating and maintaining digital platforms that support our research and consultancy services. With expertise in modern web technologies, Jay ensures our online presence effectively communicates our mission and services to clients across New Zealand, Asia, and the Pacific.

Technical Expertise

  • Front-end Web Development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript)
  • Responsive Web Design & Mobile Optimization
  • User Experience (UX) & Interface Design
  • Website Performance Optimization
  • Content Management Systems
  • Web Accessibility Standards

Key Responsibilities

  • Developing and maintaining the company website and digital platforms
  • Ensuring responsive design across all devices and browsers
  • Implementing user-friendly interfaces for research tools and resources
  • Optimizing website performance and search engine visibility
  • Supporting digital communication and online presence initiatives
  • Collaborating with the research team to present complex data effectively

Role in the Team

As the Web Developer, Jay plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between our research expertise and public accessibility. By creating intuitive, professional web platforms, Jay helps ensure that our evidence-based safety solutions and research findings are effectively communicated to potential clients, partners, and the broader community interested in occupational health and safety.

Focus Areas

Jay specializes in creating digital solutions that support our research dissemination efforts, client engagement platforms, and educational resources. This includes developing interactive tools that help organizations better understand their safety climate and implement evidence-based improvements.
