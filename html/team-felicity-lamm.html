Dr. Felicity Lamm

Senior Research Partner

About

Dr. Felicity Lamm serves as a Senior Research Partner at HEALTH&SAFETY RESEARCH CONSULT LIMITED, bringing extensive academic and practical expertise to our research initiatives. With a distinguished career in occupational health and safety research, Dr. Lamm plays a crucial role in advancing evidence-based safety solutions and contributing to the development of workplace safety standards across New Zealand and the Pacific region.

Research Focus

Dr. Lamm specializes in comprehensive workplace safety research, with particular emphasis on developing practical, evidence-based solutions that can be implemented across diverse industries. Her work contributes significantly to understanding the complex relationships between workplace culture, safety practices, and worker well-being.

Expertise

  • Occupational Health & Safety Research
  • Workplace Safety Culture Analysis
  • Academic Research Collaboration
  • Policy Development & Analysis
  • Evidence-Based Practice Implementation
  • Cross-Industry Safety Standards
  • Research Methodology & Design
  • Statistical Analysis & Interpretation

Contributions to the Team

As Senior Research Partner, Dr. Lamm provides critical oversight and guidance for major research projects. She collaborates closely with Dr. Andy Ang and the research team to ensure that all studies meet rigorous academic standards while maintaining practical applicability for client organizations. Her extensive network in academic and industry circles helps facilitate collaborative research opportunities.

Research Areas

  • Safety climate assessment and improvement strategies
  • Workplace psychosocial risk factors
  • Industry-specific safety challenges
  • Evidence-based intervention design
  • Cross-cultural workplace safety considerations
  • Regulatory compliance and best practices

Education & Qualifications

  • PhD in Occupational Health & Safety Research
  • Extensive academic and industry collaboration experience
  • Published researcher in workplace safety and health
